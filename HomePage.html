About HI-Toyz

Welcome to HI-Toyz, place where you playZ! HI-Toyz has a rich history even though it’s only been our third year. It all began in 2019, when our founder, Alves Renato Sennellius, open a small place to display his toy collections. However, after many toy collectors came to the store with the intention to buy some of Alves' collections, he decides to open HI-Toyz.

From casual intentions, HI-Toyz quickly became a beloved destination for local collectors. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, adapting to changing trends and technologies, while remaining dedicated to our core mission of providing quality toys that inspire creativity and bring smiles to everyone.

HI-Toyz only sells a few kinds of toys at first. However, due to high-demand for branded-toys, HI-Toyz decides to sell several popular branded-toys, such as Barbie, Hot Wheels, and LEGO. Over the last three years, HI-Toyz has been popular among local toy collectors and has become an official reseller for big brands such as Tamiya, Bandai, Mattel Toys, Mini-GT, Funko, and many others.

As we look to the future, HI-Toyz continues to embrace new opportunities and advancements in the toy industry. We are dedicated to stay at the forefront of toy trends, technology, and safety standards, ensuring that we provide an exceptional and memorable experience for our valued customers.

Thank you for being a part of HI-Toyz’s journey. We are thrilled to continue sharing the joy of toys with you.
